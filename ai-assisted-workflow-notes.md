# AI-Assisted Workflow Notes

Examples of where AI can support operational coordination.

## 1. Meeting Preparation
Use AI to:
- summarize meeting notes
- draft briefing materials
- surface risks and follow-ups

## 2. Scheduling Support
Use AI to:
- help evaluate scheduling conflicts
- propose prioritization options
- generate alternative scheduling scenarios

## 3. Stakeholder Communications
Use AI to support:
- drafting status updates
- summarizing decisions
- preparing follow-up communications

## 4. Workflow Documentation
Use AI to accelerate:
- SOP drafting
- process documentation
- checklist generation
